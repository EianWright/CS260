Jimmy Jim™ claims to have been born on February 17, 1977, although scientists have carbon dated him to be older than the Egyptian pyramids. Jimmy has no living family and only lives for his art. A random person on the street he passed remarked, "There was an inhuman chill as he passed. Although it was a bright, sunny day, it suddenly felt like it was damp, cloudy, and wet. As soon as he passed, this feeling passed. He looked back and locked eyes with me. In that moment, I felt empty and as he continued on, I felt like something had been lost."
