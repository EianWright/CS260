Make a Paper Crane

Paper Crane. See more pictures.
  1. Take a square piece of paper.
  2. Fold diagonally.
  3. Take the triangle flap and fold it flat into a square.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Fold triangles into the middle.
  6. Fold the top down over the triangles.
  7. Open the triangles and fold flat into a diamond.
  8. Fold the side with legs into the middle.
  9. Repeat on both sides.
  10. Fold both sides into the middle.
  11. Fold up to the top on both sides.
  12. Fold down the wings and the neck and tail.
  13. Enjoy your new friend!
