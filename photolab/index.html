Have you ever stood by the side of a lake, looked out, and thought, "Why is no one taking my picture?"


Or have you ever been in the middle of putting up tape in preparation for painting and wondered, "How stupid do I look right now?" or "How many OSHA regulations, guidelines, and rules does this break and how will I prove those were broken in my court case against my place of employment?"

Jimmy Jim™ is here to change the industry norms. He is here to shake things up and be a real force for difference in photography. He has made a personal, deep, irrevocable commitment to himself (and definitely not with the devil) to always be there for the photo. If this means breaking into your house without permission, he do it. If this means hiking for 5 days into the wilderness during an intense thunderstorm, hurricane, or blizzard without food, water or shelter, he will do it. Jimmy Jim is committed to his art and will always be there. He also swears to "never" steal your soul through photos. It's not like he needs it. He assuredly still has his (because he most certainly didn't lose it from dealing with the devil).
